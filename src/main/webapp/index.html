Introducing AGUA.SAT

Easy Data Access and Analysis

Effortlessly access and analyze relevant data from the EU Space Programme, including Copernicus and Galileo/EGNOS, to make informed decisions about plastic pollution management. Our user-friendly platform simplifies data extraction, processing, and visualization for users of all expertise levels.

Innovative Cleanup Solutions

Embrace the European Green Deal's objectives and take part in the global movement to reduce plastic pollution. AGUA SAT is dedicated to making a positive environmental impact by providing powerful tools and resources for detecting, monitoring, and removing plastic waste from our waterways.

Scalable and Adaptable Solutions

Benefit from a platform designed for scalability and adaptability, with the potential to expand its capabilities to address the unique challenges of different regions and areas. AGUA SAT is built to support new market opportunities and innovative approaches to pollution management.

Collaborative Platform for Stakeholders

Join a growing network of organizations, authorities, and individuals working together to combat plastic pollution. AGUA SAT fosters collaboration by enabling seamless data sharing and communication among stakeholders, helping to drive impactful and coordinated efforts.

Use data visualization, collaboration tools, and customizable reports. By leveraging these features, users can make informed decisions, develop targeted solutions, and take collective action to address water pollution issues.

Real-time Plastic Pollution Monitoring

Experience cutting-edge satellite technology combined with advanced AI algorithms for accurate and real-time detection of plastic pollution in rivers, lakes, shores, and coastal zones. Stay informed and take prompt action to mitigate the impacts of pollution on marine life and human health.
